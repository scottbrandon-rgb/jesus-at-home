Harrison Faith Church ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you subscribe to our Jesus at Home SMS devotional campaign.

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to Jesus at Home text messages, we collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: Required to deliver devotional messages
  • Name (Optional): To personalize greeting messages
  • Child's Name (Optional): To customize devotional content
  • Subscription Date & Time: To track enrollment and communication history
  • Message Engagement Data: Whether messages were delivered, read, or if you replied

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Delivering daily or weekly Jesus at Home devotional messages to your phone
  • Personalizing content with your name or child's name when you provide it
  • Responding to questions or support requests you send via SMS reply
  • Improving our devotional content and campaign effectiveness
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations
We will never: Sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is kept confidential within Harrison Faith Church.

3. Message Frequency & Opt-Out

Jesus at Home subscribers can expect to receive [INSERT: X messages per week/day]. Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless plan.

How to Unsubscribe

You may unsubscribe from Jesus at Home at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to our campaign shortcode or number
  • Replying QUIT or UNSUBSCRIBE to any message
  • Contacting hello@harrisonfaith.org with your request

After you unsubscribe, we will stop sending messages within 24 hours. Your contact information will be retained only as required by law or for our records.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information:

  • Your phone number and personal data are stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized Harrison Faith Church personnel
  • We use trusted SMS service providers who comply with data protection regulations
  • No sensitive financial information is collected or transmitted via SMS

While we implement security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to maintain the confidentiality of your account information.

5. Third-Party SMS Provider

Jesus at Home messages are sent through a third-party SMS service provider. This provider:

  • May store your phone number for message delivery purposes
  • Operates under their own privacy policy and data protection agreements
  • Does not use your number for other marketing campaigns without consent

We require our SMS provider to maintain strict confidentiality and use your information only as necessary to deliver your messages.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Right to Access: You can request confirmation of what personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Correct: You can request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to Unsubscribe: You can opt out of all communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@harrisonfaith.org.

7. Compliance with Regulations

Jesus at Home complies with applicable laws and regulations, including:

  • TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act): We obtain express written consent before sending SMS messages and provide clear opt-out options
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): For EU residents, your data is processed lawfully and with your consent
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): California residents have additional rights to know, delete, and opt-out as outlined above
  • Carrier Guidelines: We follow all applicable wireless carrier regulations

8. Children's Privacy

Jesus at Home is designed for parents and families. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents who subscribe on behalf of their children are responsible for monitoring their child's interaction with our messages. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided information without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Your continued subscription constitutes acceptance of updated terms. We will notify you of significant changes via text message or email when possible.
